About Me

At the end of 2014, having never laid eyes on Nashville, I packed my belongings into a van and headed south from Brooklyn, New York. I've spent much of last year doing what I knew best, traveling the country playing music - from Washington State to Key West, I've seen just about every landscape North America has to offer. However, having spent 13 years playing music for a living, I felt that It was about time to try something new and throw myself into a completely new field. Having grown interested in tech and development a couple years ago, when I heard about Nashville Software School, it seemed like a perfect opportunity.

------------

I'm enjoying the challenge of learning new skills and I'm excited to learn new technologies and expand my skill set as a web developer. I love creating effective applications that look and feel as nice on the inside as they do on the outside. I'm excited to collaborate with creative, like-minded folks who work hard, play ping pong hard, and enjoy beer or three.

Oprah Giffrey

A new animated GIF of Oprah Winfrey tweeted daily.

Oprah Giffrey is a twitter bot build using node.js. She tweets a random gif of Oprah everyday but, if you need more Oprah gifs in your life just tweet @OprahGiffrey and she will happily tweet you something special.

  • Category: Node Twitter bot project

Attic

Colaborate on photo albums with friends.

Attic is a simple to use photo sharing application that allows users to sign in and create private photo albums.
The user is able to upload photos directly from their desktop by selecting files or draggin and dropping onto the browser window. Users are also able to invite friends to join private albums allowing for multiple member upload on a single album.

  • Category: Front End project for Nashville Software School.
